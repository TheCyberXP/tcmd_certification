Rationale

'Advanced' training could mean any number of things


Basis for TCMD certification


We define advanced training as the following:


Exposing developers to the tools, APIs, and platform-specific configurations to deliver “best of breed” native mobile applications

'Advanced' is a loaded term — what's advanced to you might not be what's advanced to me, so we do the best we can to define what we mean by advanced
As a practical matter, our advanced training is the basis for TCMD
'Best of breed' means delivering the best of the target platform — deep OS integration, solid performance, and polished user interface
